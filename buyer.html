Before you continue

Ask the seller to register on VeriTrade first

Before buying from a seller, it is best to ask them to register with VeriTrade so their identity and trust profile can be checked properly. If the seller declines or is not willing to register, you can use this page to record the seller details yourself and run a basic safety check before continuing with the purchase.
